OUR TEAM

Our team consists of me and my cousins. It was just a random idea that just crossed my mind. We were pretty bored and exhausted since last year. Plus there were people who were not getting their desirable cakes that they wanted. Especially, THE VEGAN ONES! So that's it. That's all that took us to gear up ourselves and start baking. Baking was a complete new chapter for me. Being completely unsure about the entire thing, mixed emotions were running in my mind before delivering my first order. But wohooo, apparently, it turned out to be great. So! we started working harder and see where it got us. We have already delivered 600+ of our items and the feeling is out of the world.

So guys, whosoever is reading this, just give it a shot and we promise to not disappoint you a bit.
